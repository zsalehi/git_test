This is a just an article I wrote

By: Zaigham Salehi

You really are never ready.

Secretly, you aren’t ready to accomplish your dreams. You feel ready, you just somehow never accomplish your goals. Here’s how I learned that I am never ready.

Recognizing the problem

Realizing I even had a problem probably took me the longest to understand, but once I did it opened a window into my life which was hidden away in my mind behind some curtains. I love technology, and becoming a programmer has always been a goal of mine ever since I decided to choose business over computer science back in high school.

Why did I make that choice?

I honestly can’t remember exactly when I thought to change my path, but I think I chose business because I always felt comfortable with understanding how a company works and that I want to run my own someday. That’s the word! Comfortable.

After a whole lot of effort trying to learn to code over a span of maybe four years, I definitely understand programming way better than I did in high school but I’m not any better at it really. How could that be? This question was one I struggled with answering. I did not know what I was doing wrong. But then it hit me.
